L3 SOD

This course explores the complex and dynamic world of software development. You will delve into the methodologies, tools, and practices that drive successful software projects.

Students will gain hands-on experience with the latest development environments, version control systems, and testing frameworks, ensuring they are well-prepared for industry demands.

Critical thinking and problem-solving skills will be honed as students tackle real-world challenges, learning to develop robust and scalable software solutions.

The curriculum also emphasizes collaboration and communication, vital skills for working effectively in development teams and interacting with stakeholders.

Students will have the opportunity to engage in projects that simulate professional environments, providing a valuable bridge between theory and practice.

By the end of the course, students will have a comprehensive understanding of the software development lifecycle and be ready to contribute to innovative technology solutions.

L3 CSA

The Computer Systems Architecture course provides a deep understanding of how computer systems operate at a fundamental level. You'll explore the hardware and software that form the backbone of computing.

Topics include processor design, memory hierarchy, input/output systems, and the integration of these components to create efficient and powerful computers.

Students will learn about different types of processors, including RISC and CISC architectures, and how these designs impact performance and energy efficiency.

Memory management and optimization techniques will be covered, giving students insights into how data is stored, accessed, and manipulated within a computer system.

Practical sessions will provide hands-on experience with assembly language programming and hardware simulation tools, enhancing theoretical knowledge with practical skills.

By the end of the course, students will have a strong grasp of computer architecture principles, preparing them for careers in hardware design, systems engineering, and related fields.

L3 BDC

Big Data and Cloud Computing is a cutting-edge course designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to navigate the fast-evolving landscape of data and cloud technologies.

You'll learn about the principles and practices of big data analytics, including data collection, storage, processing, and visualization using contemporary tools and platforms.

The course covers cloud computing models such as IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS, and explores how these models are transforming the way businesses operate and scale their IT infrastructure.

Students will gain hands-on experience with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, learning to deploy and manage applications in cloud environments.

Security and privacy concerns in big data and cloud computing will be addressed, preparing students to design and implement secure, compliant systems.

By the end of the course, students will be proficient in big data and cloud computing technologies, ready to take on roles as data scientists, cloud architects, and IT professionals.

L3 TRS

Telecommunications and Network Security is a comprehensive course that delves into the technologies and practices that ensure secure communication over networks.

Students will learn about network protocols, encryption methods, and security measures that protect data transmission from eavesdropping, tampering, and other threats.

The course covers the architecture of modern telecommunications systems, including wired and wireless networks, and the challenges of securing these systems.
